Aero Tuning

If the vehicle is tuned properly it should NOT require aero at all.
Drive the vehicle around first to see if it's needed.
If aero IS added, set front and rear to the lowest setting to start.

Guidelines
  • Understeering: Add small amount to front
  • Rear Sliding: Add small amount to rear
Tuning Logic
  • Front: More turn grip, less top speed
  • Rear: More corner grip, less top speed
  • Both: Max grip, way less top speed
Pro-Tip

DO NOT use Aero at all! IF FORCED, use very little!
