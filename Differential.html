Differential Tuner

There is no "magic formula" for diffs—it relies on car power and feel.
The golden rule: Start low and add as needed to manage wheelspin.

General / RWD Baseline
  • Rear Accel: 30% – 50%
  • Rear Decel: 15% – 30%
  • Higher Accel = More Oversteer
AWD Specific Setup
  • Front: 30% Accel / 10% Decel
  • Rear: 50% Accel / 20% Decel
  • Center Bias: 70% – 80% (Rear)
The "Start Low" Methodology
  1. Set Accel to 20–30% and Decel to 10–15%.
  2. Test corner exits. If the inside tire spins, raise Accel in +5% increments.
  3. For AWD, set Front Accel higher (60–80%) if you need the car to "pull" straight.
