Alignment Tuner

In Forza, optimal camber is determined by using in-game telemetry to make the outside tire's temperature even across the tread during cornering.
The goal: Make the tire surface flat on the ground at full corner load.

Optimal Targets
  • Front: -0.5 to -2.5 degrees
  • Rear: 0 to -1.5 degrees
Front vs. Rear Strategy
  • Turning: More negative front camber
  • Stability: Less negative rear camber
The Telemetry Method
  • Open telemetry to the Tire Temperature screen.
  • Drive a technical track; check temps at the apex of corners.
  • Inside Hotter: Too much negative camber (move closer to 0).
  • Outside Hotter: Not enough negative camber (add more).
Caster Influence

Increasing caster (usually to 5.0 - 7.0) adds more negative camber when turning, allowing for less static camber on the straights.

DRIFT SETUP: Front -5° | Rear 0° to -0.5°
