Brake Tuner

Braking in Forza relies on maximizing weight transfer and managing tire grip.
The goal is to apply maximum pressure in a straight line before a turn, then release gradually while turning.

Standard Setup
  • Pressure: 100% – 130%
  • Bias: 45% – 55%
  • Pro Pressure: 110% – 120%
  • Bias: 47% – 49%
Pro Techniques
  • ABS Off: Press less peddle to avoid lock-up.
  • Modulation: Hardest in; Soft out.
  • Braking Line: Turn on braking line assist.
  • Do it 4 Dale: More gas; counter steer.
Tuning Impact
  • Forward Bias (48-52%): Increases stability during heavy braking.
  • Rearward Bias: Helps the car rotate into tight corners.
