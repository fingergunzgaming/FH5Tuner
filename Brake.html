Brake Tuner

Braking in Forza relies on maximizing weight transfer and managing tire grip.
The goal: Apply maximum pressure in a straight line before a turn, then release gradually while turning.

Standard Setup
  • Pressure: 100% – 130%
  • Bias: 45% – 55%
  • Pro Pressure: 110% – 120%
  • Pro Bias: 47% – 49%
Pro Techniques
  • ABS Off: Modulate pedal to avoid lock-up
  • Modulation: Hard entry; Soft release
  • Assists: Use braking line to find markers
  • Do it 4 Dale: More gas; counter steer
Tuning Impact
  • Forward Bias (48-52%): Increases stability during heavy braking.
  • Rearward Bias: Helps the car rotate into tight corners.
